Street Photography

What I love most about street photography is that you are treating the whole world as a playground developing and creating countless possibilities before your own eyes. It's quite a difficult endeavour because of its fast paced and uncontrolled nature, but in my eyes these are also the reasons why it's the most rewarding one. My mission as a street photographer is to shine some light on those sudden moments and encounters that most people miss in their day to day lives.
