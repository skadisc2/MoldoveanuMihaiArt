About

My name is Moldoveanu Mihai and I'm a visual artist based in Cluj-Napoca. My photography work mainly explores my relationship with finding interest and beauty in the little things in life. Saul Leiter once said that a window covered in raindrops interests him more than photographing a famous person and I have to say that's also my truth.

Equipment

Film 

The Yashica Mat 124-G is one of the reasons I will never stop shooting on medium format film. Just using this camera is an immense pleasure for me, let alone the fact that I took some of my favorite shots with it.

Digital

I've always been a bit of a Nikon fanboy, and recently I upgraded to the Nikon Z6 which is more than I could have ever wished for. When it comes to the glass,I'm only using the 50mm Nikkor 1.8S.

3D Visuals

I know a lot of people are afraid to get into 3D work because of the spec requirements, but right now a station that can handle complex tasks in 3D is not thaaaat expensive anymore. I'm using a HP Omen 15 with 8th gen i7 and 16gb of ram and I can definetely reccommend it for beginners.
