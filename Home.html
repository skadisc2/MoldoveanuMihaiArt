No Signal Music

Entertainment company based in Brasov, Romania. Our goal is to make parties in beautiful locations.
We started making events in 2019 and we've connected with different kind of people and magical places. We hope that our events portfolio expends all over the country and someday worldwide. Making unforgettable moments it's our priority. We desire to see you to as many as possible events powder by us.

Create Your Event

We organize your festival-style party.
All you have to do is bring your friends or family to have
fun.
If you dreamed about having a festival-like party, now it's
possible.
We take care of all the details and guarantee that the
services are offered by the providers and suppliers of the
well-known festivals in Romania.
Included services:
- 3 DJs;
- 1 well-known guest DJ;
- sound system at least 10,000 W;
- dj desk with led + led screens;
- stage & architectural lights;
- CO2 or flames;
- generator;
- tent event or booking a unique location;
- event logistics;
